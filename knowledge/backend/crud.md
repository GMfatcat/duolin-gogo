## zh-TW

很多 API 或資料後端的最基本能力，就是建立、讀取、更新與刪除資源。
雖然真實系統會比這更複雜，但 CRUD 常是理解資源操作的第一層骨架。

## en

Many APIs and data-backed systems start from the ability to create, read, update, and delete resources.
Real systems are more complex than that, but CRUD is often the first useful mental model.
