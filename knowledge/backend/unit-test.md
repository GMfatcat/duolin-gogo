## zh-TW

unit test 通常只測一小塊邏輯，例如某個函式、方法或轉換規則。
它的價值是快、穩，並且能讓你快速知道局部邏輯有沒有壞掉。

## en

A unit test usually covers a small piece of logic such as a function, method, or transformation rule.
Its value is speed, stability, and fast feedback on local behavior.
