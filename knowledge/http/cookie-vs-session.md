## zh-TW

cookie 是瀏覽器儲存的一小段資料，session 則常指伺服器端維護的登入或狀態資訊。
它們常一起運作，但角色並不相同。

## en

A cookie is a small piece of data stored by the browser, while a session often refers to server-side state such as login tracking.
They often work together, but they do not mean the same thing.
