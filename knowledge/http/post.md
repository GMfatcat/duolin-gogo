## zh-TW

`POST` 常見於建立新資源、提交表單，或觸發某個需要伺服器處理的動作。
它通常代表「把資料送進去做事」，而不只是單純讀取。

## en

`POST` is commonly used for creating resources, submitting forms, or triggering some server-side action.
It usually means "send data in so something happens" rather than simply reading data.
