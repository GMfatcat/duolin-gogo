## zh-TW

`GET` 是最常見的 HTTP method 之一，通常用來請求某個資源的內容。
它的典型用途是讀取資料，而不是建立、修改或刪除資料。

## en

`GET` is one of the most common HTTP methods and is usually used to request the contents of a resource.
Its typical role is reading data, not creating, updating, or deleting it.
