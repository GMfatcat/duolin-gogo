## zh-TW

TLS 會在傳輸過程中幫資料加密，讓中途竊聽者較難直接讀出內容。
它也和憑證驗證有關，所以不只是單純「把字變亂」而已。

## en

TLS encrypts data in transit so eavesdroppers have a much harder time reading the contents directly.
It also relates to certificate-based authenticity, so it is more than just scrambling bytes.
