## zh-TW

`lambda` 常用來建立簡短的一次性函式，特別是在排序、映射或過濾時很常看到。
它適合很小的邏輯，但通常不適合塞進太複雜的內容。
可以把它想成臨時的小函式，而不是完整展開的函式定義。

## en

`lambda` is commonly used for short one-off functions, especially in sorting, mapping, or filtering.
It works best for small pieces of logic and usually becomes hard to read if it grows too complex.
You can think of it as a temporary compact function rather than a fully expanded definition.
