## zh-TW

`COUNT(*)` 常見於統計總筆數、分組計數與報表查詢。
它的重點是數目前結果中有多少列，而不是挑某一個欄位內容來看。

## en

`COUNT(*)` commonly appears in total counts, grouped summaries, and reporting queries.
Its job is to count how many rows exist in the current result set rather than inspect one specific column value.
