## zh-TW

`SELECT` 是最常見的查詢語句，用來從資料表取出你要看的欄位資料。
它本身偏向讀取，不是在更新或刪除資料。

## en

`SELECT` is the most common query statement and is used to retrieve the columns you want to inspect from a table.
It is mainly about reading data, not updating or deleting it.
