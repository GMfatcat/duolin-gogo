## zh-TW

`map` 偏向把每個元素轉成另一種值，`filter` 偏向保留符合條件的元素，`reduce` 則把整個集合收斂成一個結果。
它們常讓資料處理邏輯更接近「我要什麼」，而不是「我怎麼一步步做」。

## en

`map` transforms each element, `filter` keeps matching elements, and `reduce` collapses a collection into one result.
They often make code read more like "what I want" than "how I manually step through it."
