## zh-TW

在物件導向設計裡，class 常用來定義物件有哪些欄位與方法。
它比較像規格或藍圖，而不是實際已經存在的一個具體實例。

## en

In object-oriented design, a class often defines what fields and methods an object has.
It is more like a specification or blueprint than a concrete instance that already exists.
